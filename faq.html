Overrated or Nah is a site dedicated to answering a simple question: Is this overrated, or nah? Places to eat, the latest tech, fashion trends, cities around the world, we want to know how you really feel. Simply click one of the three options below to cast your votes on whether you feel something is overrated or underrated. To see the most up-to-date power rankings, click the trophy icon to the left.

Overrated or Nah was founded by Aaron Tian, Matia Mearns, Juan Pablo Quintero, Brette Connolly, and Chahat Chadha and built with huge help from William Holley IV. We are currently based out of New York City, though we’re looking to represent the whole world. If you have any tips, comments, or suggestions on how we can improve the site, we’d love to hear it!
