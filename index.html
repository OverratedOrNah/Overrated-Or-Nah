Welcome to Overrated or Nah!
We want to know how you feel about pop trends and culture right now by asking a simple question about it: Is it overrated or nah?
You’ll be given a slideshow of different topics, everything from Kanye West to PETA to Chipotle, and have 3 options to choose from: Overrated, Underrated, or No Preference.
(Don’t worry about hurting Kanye’s feelings, all of your answers will be totally anonymous)
Our game topics fall into 7 categories: People, Trends, Fashion, Restaurants, Tech, Places, and Other. To see what we’ve deemed the most overrated and underrated things in each category based on the information you’ve given us, see “Rankings” below.
Click “Play” in the bar above to start the game!
