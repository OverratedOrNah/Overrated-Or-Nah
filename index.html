Overrated or Nah is a culture site dedicated to answering the age-old question: Is this overrated, or nah? Places to eat, the latest tech, fashion trends, cities around the world, we want to know if you feeling some type of way.
Simply click one of the three options (Overrated, Underrated, or IDK) to cast your votes. We keep track of all the data and let you know what's hot.
To see the most up-to-date power rankings, click "Rankings" above.
